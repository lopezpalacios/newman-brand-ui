Construction & Clear Space

Lorem ipsum dolor sit.

Clear space equals the height of one isotype bar (X) on every side. Never place type, imagery or other logos inside this zone.

X X
Minimum size

To preserve the fine descending bars, never reproduce the full lockup below 32 mm / 120 px wide. The standalone isotype may go as small as 16 px.

Composition

Isotype and wordmark are locked in proportion and alignment. Never recreate, re-space or re-scale them independently — always use the master files.
